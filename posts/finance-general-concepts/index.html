Credit ratings - Some thoughts on Issuer Rating and Seniority

So basically you would have to check a little bit more into the detail on how these issuer ratings are defined…

I started this exploration as I wanted to understand if the seniority of a bond is included in the rating being a quite important factor..

I ended up here - see link with last paragraph above.

So you see that the first important distinction that you have to understand… understand always if a person is talking about an issuer rating or a particular security rating.

These can be quite different.

Note now that these ratings are like the University rankings. In themselves they do not tell you anything. You have to check how they are composed if you really want to understand the absolute number / score.

It is the concept of aggregation of information where then ultimately a ton of information is lost. Again can use them as a very rough indicator but obviously if you know how they are composed and you have a little bit of a clue about what you are interested in you could come up with something more tailored.

make that research in this sense…. would be cool to check at the methodologies of these…

of course this would be a different job… but I guess that it is good to have a little bit of understanding in this dimension given the importance of these securities for the business.
